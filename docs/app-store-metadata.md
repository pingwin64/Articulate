## Description

Read one word at a time. Build real focus.

Articulate strips away the noise of traditional reading. Each word appears alone — no page, no paragraph, no distractions. Just you and the text. It's a deceptively simple idea that transforms how you read.

WHAT YOU GET
- One-word-at-a-time reading with adjustable speed
- 30+ curated texts: classic literature, speeches, poetry, philosophy, and more
- Comprehension quizzes powered by AI
- Pronunciation practice with instant feedback
- Word bank to save and review vocabulary
- Daily streaks, achievements, and reading insights

PERSONALIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE
- 5 fonts including OpenDyslexic for accessibility
- Light, dark, and wind-down modes
- Adjustable word size, color, and background themes
- Set a daily reading goal and track your progress

BUILT FOR YOUR BRAIN
Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) isn't just a reading trick — it's backed by cognitive science. By eliminating saccadic eye movement, your brain processes language more efficiently. Many readers find they comprehend more while reading faster.

ADD YOUR OWN TEXT
Paste articles, scan pages with your camera, or import PDFs. Read anything the way your brain prefers.

ARTICULATE PRO
Unlock all fonts, colors, themes, unlimited text imports, text-to-speech, chunk reading, and the full word bank with flashcard review.
